Choice

Yes, it is feedback loops!

The ability to make choices and adjust based on feedback is what defines a cybernetic system. The term cyber is derived from the greek word "Kybernetes" meaning to steer a ship. Cybernetic systems function similarly.

Sense and Correct

Of course you are! This is because you can sense your environment and make corrections to achieve a desired objective. For example:

Find the hidden message at the center of the sine wave.

To find the hidden message you probably overshot the movement slightly and had to adjust until you achieved the objective. This ability to sense and adapt is fundamental to Cybernetics systems.

Self-Regulation

One of the key elements of a cybernetic system is that it is also self regulating. For example, all of the neurons in your brain function without direct instructions from you.

Whereas something like a musicbox is not self-regulating.

Try clicking on the music box.

Note how the dancing figurine is not self regulating or adaptive. It doesn't sense anything therefore it can only do what it was directly programmed to do.
