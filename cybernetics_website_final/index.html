Have a seat. It's time for your examination on Cybernetics. Yes I am Norbert Wiener, but let's not get hung up on that. You're first question is...



Name this system:

Choice

You're ability to make a choice, and adjust is what makes you a cybernetic system. The term cybernetics is derived from a greek word meaning to steer a ship. In that sense you, and most functional systems, steer themselves back on track by making adjustments based on feedback. For example make a choice from the following options:

Sense and Correct

Notice how in order to find the hidden message you had to take several attempts. You overshot the movement slightly and then had to adjust by going slower until you achieved your objective. This ability to sense and adapt to your environment is part of my understanding of Cybernetics.
Try making some interesting patterns with the sine wave. You will notice that same process of adjustment until you reach the desired pattern.
