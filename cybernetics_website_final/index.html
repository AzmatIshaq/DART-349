One of the key elements of a cybernetic system is that it is self regulating. For example, all of your neurons in your brains are operating without you needing to do anything. Furthermore they are assembling themselves according to input.

One way to think about this is to imagine the operation of a musicboc. The dancing figurine on top of the music box is not self regulating or adaptive. It doesn't sense anything and therefore can only do what it is directly programmed to do.
